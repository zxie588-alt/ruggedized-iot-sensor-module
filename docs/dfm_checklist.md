## Manufacturing Risks

| Risk | Why It Matters | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Gasket compression mismatch | Direct ingress risk | Add section drawing and compression target |
| Boss cracking | Loss of seal compression | Add torque limit and boss rib detail |
| Strap lug stress concentration | Device may detach outdoors | Add fillet/radius refinement and FEA iteration |
| Service port leakage | Maintenance feature becomes ingress path | Add separate seal and visual inspection |
| Battery movement | Intermittent power, enclosure damage | Add restraint clip and foam/pad note |

## Evidence Boundary

This DFM checklist supports concept design review only. It does not claim mould-tool readiness, supplier review, production quote or design-for-injection-moulding sign-off.
