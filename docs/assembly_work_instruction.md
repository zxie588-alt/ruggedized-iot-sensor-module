# Assembly Work Instruction - Concept Release

Document status: portfolio concept work instruction, not a production-controlled SOP.

## Purpose

Define a repeatable assembly sequence for the SolidWorks CAD V2 enclosure so that gasket placement, PCB location, battery restraint and service-port sealing are checked consistently.

## Tools and Parts

- Lower enclosure.
- PCB assembly or PCB envelope placeholder.
- Battery pack or battery envelope placeholder.
- Elastomer gasket.
- Upper lid.
- M3 stainless fasteners.
- Service/debug port cover.
- Visual inspection checklist.
- Low-torque hand driver for future prototype work.

## Assembly Sequence

1. Inspect lower enclosure for debris in gasket groove and screw-boss areas.
2. Place PCB on standoff locations and confirm alignment with mounting markers.
3. Place battery pack in pocket and confirm restraint clip engagement.
4. Fit gasket into the groove path, including service-port and cable-gland regions.
5. Check that the gasket is continuous and not twisted.
6. Place upper lid over alignment lip.
7. Insert fasteners by hand.
8. Tighten in a cross pattern to avoid uneven gasket compression.
9. Inspect sensor membrane area, service/debug port cover and cable-gland clearance.
10. Record assembly issues in the validation log.

## Inspection Points

| Check | Acceptance Intent | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Gasket continuity | No visible gap, twist or pinch | Photo / inspection note |
| PCB seating | Board sits flat on standoffs | Visual check |
| Battery restraint | Battery cannot move freely | Manual shake observation |
| Screw pattern | Cross-pattern tightening followed | Assembly checklist |
| Service port | Cover and seal path seated | Visual check |

## Evidence Boundary

This work instruction is based on CAD V2. It has not yet been validated with a physical prototype or torque-controlled assembly trial.
